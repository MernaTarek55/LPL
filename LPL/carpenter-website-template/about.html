About Us

Our project leverages advanced facial recognition technology with aging simulation to efficiently locate missing individuals. By generating updated images that reflect changes over time, our system improves the accuracy and speed of identification. Users can input older photos and current ages to create age-progressed images, which are then matched against a comprehensive database. This innovative approach aims to enhance search efforts, offering hope and closure to families and communities affected by the disappearance of their loved ones.
